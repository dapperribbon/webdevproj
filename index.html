About the event



Some of the highlights in this segment include a Display of capabilities by the SAF and the Home Team, marching contingents from the uniformed services, a virtual military tattoo, and a virtual choir performance of the NDP 2022 theme song “Stronger Together”.

Followed by the Show segment titled “The Story of Us”. Which will include live performances and a show film, depicting how Singapore, as a community and nation, emerges stronger from challenges that we have faced. Singaporeans can expect a meaningful show that celebrates the strength and unity of Singaporeans, and calls for us to rise together.

About NDP

The Nation Day of Singapore is celebrated every year on August 9, in commemoration of Singapore's independence from Malaysia in 1965. This holiday features a Parade, an address by the Prime Minister of Singapore, and various celebrations.
